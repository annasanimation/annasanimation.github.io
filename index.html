— 02

Director·Stop-Motion·Diploma Work

How to Shade Silence

"A simple question can move the light inside silence."

A 3-minute experimental stop-motion short film inspired by Sergey Kozlov's childhood fairytale. Each character and background was created on separate transparent sheets of plexiglass, photographed frame by frame on a light table.

The hedgehog and bear were built in layers of watercolour on separate plexiglass sheets. Backgrounds were painted and shifted frame by frame to create depth and parallax motion.
